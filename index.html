Pet-Friendly

Welcome Four-Legged Guests

Our dog-friendly rooms offer treats, bowls, and access to nearby Piccadilly Gardens for walks. Dogs must be on leads in public areas and supervised (max two dogs, £20/night per pet, service dogs free).
