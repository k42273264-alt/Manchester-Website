Manchester Adventures

Experience Manchester’s Energy

From the iconic Old Trafford to the cutting-edge Co-op Live, Manchester blends rich sporting heritage with vibrant cultural and music scenes. Stay at Manchester Portland by Sunday, just steps from Piccadilly Gardens, and explore nearby attractions like Etihad Stadium, MCC, and Aviva Studios, perfect for a memorable city getaway.

Discover Manchester


Science and Industry Museum

The Science and Industry Museum in Manchester showcases historic industrial innovations and interactive exhibits celebrating world-changing ideas.

Etihad Stadium

Home to Manchester City, this modern stadium is a 10-minute drive or tram ride, with tours and events year-round.

Co-op Live

Manchester’s newest entertainment venue, hosting concerts and events, accessible via a short tram ride from Piccadilly Station.
