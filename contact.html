Getting Here

Travel to Manchester Portland by Sunday

Nearest station: Manchester Piccadilly – 0.3 miles / 5-minute walk.

Local buses connect to Piccadilly Gardens, opposite the hotel.

The hotel is opposite Piccadilly Gardens.

Nearest airport: Manchester Airport – 7 miles / 15-minute train to Piccadilly Station.
